Machine learning

The data trains itself into edge

Every trade we collect is a labeled training example. Tokens that 10x'd, tokens that rugged, wallets that won, wallets that lost. After months of collection, we have something no ML team can buy: ground truth at scale. And we're not locked to one model — we run GPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok in parallel, each with different strengths: Twitter/X access, reasoning depth, speed, cost efficiency. Full-spectrum AI analysis on every coin.

Pattern Recognition Model
Train on thousands of token lifecycles. The model sees the first 10 trades of a new token and says: "This looks 84% like tokens that ran to $2M+" or "This signature matches known rug patterns." It learns what humans can't see — subtle correlations across 50+ features simultaneously.
Smart Entry Detection
Big news drops. A new coin appears. The model sees: 5 wallets with 75%+ win rates just bought, viewer count spiking, buy pressure accelerating, signature matches previous runners. Confidence: high. Risk:reward ratio computed in real time. The bot enters before the crowd even notices.
Risk Management & R:R Engine
This is where it gets serious. The model doesn't just find entries — it manages risk. Position sizing based on confidence score. Dynamic stop losses based on token volatility profile. Automatic profit-taking at statistically optimal points. Expected R:R computed for every trade before entry. No gambling. Math.
Perpetuals Expansion
Same intelligence, applied to perps. Funding rate arbitrage detection. Liquidation cascade prediction. Long/short signals based on wallet flow analysis. The spot data informs the perps strategy — we see the smart money move on spot before it shows up in perps pricing.
Continuous Learning
The model improves every day. Every trade outcome is new training data. Market regimes shift — the model adapts. What worked last month gets validated or deprecated automatically. The longer it runs, the sharper it gets. Compounding intelligence.
